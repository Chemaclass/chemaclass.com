## Analytics

This site uses [Cronitor RUM](https://cronitor.io/real-user-monitoring) to collect anonymous usage statistics. This helps me understand how visitors use the site and improve the content.

**Data collected includes:**

- Approximate geographic location (country/region)

This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

This site stores your theme preference (light/dark mode) in your browser's local storage. This is not a cookie and is not transmitted to any server.

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:
- Know what data is collected about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of tracking

Since Cronitor RUM collects anonymized data, there is no personal data to delete. You can block analytics by using a browser extension like uBlock Origin or by disabling JavaScript.
